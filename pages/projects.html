zorm

Introduction

zorm is being made to make my class note conversion from Markdown to HTML that much easier. While I know that there are other converters out there, I want to make my own. As of right now, zorm will be a top-down parser of Markdown. It will convert Markdown to HTML as it goes through the file.

Why Use this?

Instead of using a GUI editor that forces you to leave the terminal that we all know and love, why not use a simple script that will convert your Markdown documents into HTML so it can be viewed across every operating system to avoid installing an editor to look at your pretty Markdown? There is your reason.

Usage

./zorm inputfile.md outputfile.html

wolfpack

Introduction

This colorscheme is heavily inspired by Bliss by Saad Quadri. I added my own flair to the theme to make it, in my opinion, better. I use a modified version of n0tch2k for terminal colors. I only changed this minorly, but it fits the scheme. I have included both an itermcolors and Xresources of said modded version of n0tch2k.
